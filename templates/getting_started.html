GETTING STARTED


From navigating the TA process to enrolling in college.


(Disclaimer: The following are not all inclusive instructions for every navy command and their local instructions take precedent over any information presented here)


Navigating the TA process and completing all of the steps to enroll in college to start your journey through higher education can be intimidating. Hopefully this consolidated list of instructions, links, and accompanying checklist can assist you in getting through the process.


First stop in your journey should be the Navy College website (https://www.navycollege.navy.mil/).

A site you should become intimately familiar with is the MyNavy Education website (https://myeducation.netc.navy.mil/webta/home.html#nbb). This site requires login with either DEERS account information or a CAC. This page shows you your eligibility for TA, as well as being the site you use to submit your electronic TA requests and receive your approved TA vouchers.

Obtaining your Joint Services Transcript (JST) is relatively simple. Head to the site (https://jst.doded.mil/jst/), log in and get an electronic copy of your unofficial transcript. If your school requests an official transcript be sent, you can also conduct that transaction here

If you have CLEP, AP, DSST, SAT, or ACT scores you need to submit, you can access those from the College Board (https://www.collegeboard.org/). If you are interested in taking CLEP or DSST examinations, those are discussed more in depth in the Alternative College Credit section of this website.

Complete Once During your Navy Career:
TA/NCPACE DL Policy and Procedures Training.
Navy Virtual Counseling 101 training IMI.
Schedule 1st virtual counseling with NCVEC.
Selecting a College and Degree Plan:
Select multiple colleges you are interested in.
Select multiple degree plans from all chosen colleges.
E-Mail advisors from selected schools. Tell them which degree plans you are interested in.
Send your JST and any other transcripts you have to your advisors for evaluation.
Next Steps:
Schedule second counseling with NCVEC to review degree plan.
Enroll with chosen school.
Register for classes.
Route TA request at least 14 days before class start date.
Sign NCVEC approved voucher
Submit approved and signed TA voucher through your school's process
START CLASSES!
